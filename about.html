About NavAgri Solutions

Our journey, mission, and commitment to farmers

Our Story

Founded in 2023, NavAgri Solutions began as a small farm equipment dealership in Eldoret. Our founder, a third-generation farmer, recognized the challenges farmers faced in accessing quality machinery, healthy livestock, and reliable services at fair prices.

What started as a modest operation has grown into one of Rift Valley's most trusted agricultural solutions providers, serving thousands of farmers across multiple regions.

Today, we're proud to offer comprehensive solutions that address every aspect of modern farming needs.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with high quality resources, knowledge, and support to enhance productivity, profitability, and sustainability in agriculture.

Our Vision

To transform Kenya's agriculture by bridging the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technological solutions.
